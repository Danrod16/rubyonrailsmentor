Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Ruby on Rails Mentor ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by BRIGHTWORKS DIGITAL PTE. LTD., is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mentorship services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Billing information
  • Professional background
  • Development skill level

Technical Information

  • Code snippets and projects shared during mentorship
  • Support ticket contents
  • Call recordings (with your consent)
  • Learning progress and assessments

Usage Information

  • Service usage patterns
  • Communication preferences
  • Interaction history
  • Website analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our mentorship services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Communicate about your account and services
  • Send educational materials and updates
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve our teaching methods
  • Ensure service quality and security

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Payment processors (for billing purposes)
  • Communication platforms (for mentorship calls)
  • Analytics providers (in aggregate form)
  • Legal authorities (when required by law)

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Code and Project Privacy

Regarding your code and projects:

  • Your code remains your intellectual property
  • We maintain confidentiality of your projects
  • Code examples may be used anonymously for teaching
  • You control what code you share during mentorship

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls for staff
  • Secure data storage

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website usage
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain session security

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred and processed in countries where we operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Significant changes will be notified via email.
